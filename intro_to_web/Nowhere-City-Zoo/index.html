About Baboons

Baboons are highly social primates that live in large groups called troops. They are known for their intelligence, complex social behaviors, and strong family bonds. Our zoo is home to a lively troop where you can watch their playful interactions.

Fun Facts

  • Baboons live in Africa and Arabia.
  • They use over 30 different vocalizations to communicate.
  • A troop can have up to 150 members!
  • They are omnivores, eating fruits, plants, and small animals.
